Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add games to my collection?

You can search for games manually using the search function, or use the AI shelf scanner to photograph your game shelf and automatically detect titles.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your data is stored securely with row-level security, meaning only you can access your own collection. All communication is encrypted.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device. On iOS, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. On Android, go to Google Play Store > Subscriptions.

Can I use the app without a subscription?

Yes. Dragon Hoard is free to use with up to 5 games, daily scans, and full gamification. Optional premium tiers (Hoarder and Dragon Lord) unlock higher limits and extra features.

The shelf scanner didn't recognize a game. What can I do?

AI recognition works best with clear, well-lit photos of game spines or covers. If a game isn't detected, you can always add it manually through the search function.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly in the app under Settings. Alternatively, contact us at hoardgamecollection@gmail.com and we will delete your account and all associated data.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Send us an email at hoardgamecollection@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your device model. Screenshots help!
