Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2025

Dragon Hoard ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Dragon Hatchery. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. You may also start with an anonymous account that does not require any personal information.

Game Collection Data: Information you enter about your game collection, including game titles, platforms, play time, prices, ratings, and review notes. This data is stored in your personal account.

Profile Data: Your in-app profile data such as your dragon name, experience points, streaks, and progression data.

Images (Camera): If you use the shelf-scan feature, images are temporarily processed to identify game titles. Images are sent to our server for AI analysis and are not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (game tracking, collection management)
  • To process shelf-scan images using AI (Google Gemini) for game identification
  • To look up game metadata from IGDB (Internet Game Database)
  • To manage your subscription through RevenueCat
  • To sync your collection across devices

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – Authentication and database hosting. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat – Subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API – AI image analysis for shelf scanning. Images are processed and not retained. Terms
  • IGDB (Twitch) – Game metadata lookup. No personal data is shared. Privacy Policy
  • Google ML Kit – On-device text recognition. Processing happens locally on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with row-level security policies ensuring only you can access your own data. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

API keys and secrets are stored server-side and are never exposed to the client application.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (game collection, profile) will be permanently removed from our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Dragon Hoard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your game collection data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
